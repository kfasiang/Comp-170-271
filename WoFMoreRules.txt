MORE RULES:
When you spin the wheel, you can land on any amount of money from $500 to $2500--
But be advised: you may also land on bankrupt and lose everything.
Also, if you spin the wheel and guess a consonant that is not in the puzzle
You will lose the value of the spin.
However, with this risk comes great reward! If the consonant you guess is in the puzzle,
Your spin earning will be multiplied by the number of times the consonant appears!

When you buy a vowel, exactly $250 is deducted from your bank
Regardless of how many times that vowel appears.
You will also be told when there are no vowels left.

When solving the puzzle, be careful to match all spelling and spacing correctly!

Type "go" to start the game!
